Research

My research interests broadly lie in AI for Healthcare, at the intersection of machine learning (ML), computer vision (CV), medical image analysis (MedIA), explainable AI (XAI), and agentic AI.

  • (ML, CV & MedIA). I am interested in label-efficient medical image analysis, including weakly/semi-supervised learning, representation learning, multi-modal fusion, and foundation models for medical image segmentation, reconstruction, and classification, particularly when expert annotations are scarce, imperfect, noisy, or heterogeneous across clinical sites.
  • (XAI & Agentic AI). I also study trustworthy and self-evolving AI systems for clinical reasoning, with a focus on neuro-symbolic LLM reasoning and multi-agent collaboration for radiology report generation, mental health diagnosis, and personalized care planning.
